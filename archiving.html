What is WPILL?

First established in 1992, WPILL is a journal for undergraduate and graduate students in the GMU English Department who are seeking to bring their work to publication in academic journals.

How does it work?

Through a rigorous peer review process (by your actual peers), WPILL seeks to familiarize students with the academic publication process. Published once a year, the journal is open to all topics related to languge use and utilizes both APA and MLA formats.
