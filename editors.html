Editors

Benjamin Hunt

Senior Editor

Benjamin is a Postdoctoral fellow at George Mason University in the Linguistics program. His research interests include lexicography, language documentation and reclamation, language endangerment, quantification, and language pedagogy. He earned a BS in Interdisciplinary Studies (Modern Languages and Linguistics) from the University of Central Florida, and an MA and PhD in Linguistics from GMU.

Isaac Newton Nyatuame

Isaac Newton Nyatuame is a PhD student in the Writing and Rhetoric program at George Mason University. His research interests include social justice, user localization, critical AI studies, and peace rhetoric. He holds an MA in English (Rhetoric and Professional Communication) from New Mexico State University, an MPhil in Peace and Development Studies, and a BA in Communication Studies from the University of Cape Coast in Ghana.
