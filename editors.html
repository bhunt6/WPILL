Editors

Benjamin Hunt

Benjamin is a PhD student at George Mason University in the Linguistics program. His research interests include syntax, language documentation and reclamation, language endangerment, quantification, language pedagogy, and lexicography. He earned a BS in Interdisciplinary Studies (Modern Languages and Linguistcs) from the University of Central Florida and an MA in Linguistics from GMU.

Jonathan M. Marine

Jonathan Marine is a PhD student at George Mason University in the Writing and Rhetoric program. His research interests include content analysis, writing engagement, longitudinal writing development, the pedagogy and theory of James Moffett, and the rhetoric of graffiti. He holds an MA in British Literature, a BS in English, and a BA in Psychology.
